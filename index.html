Our Story

Lumina Brew isn't just a cafe; it's a culinary journey. Born from a passion for bridging traditional Nigerian heritage with modern coffee culture, we've created a sanctuary in Lagos primarily designed for community and comfort.

We source the finest local ingredients for our Jollof and Suya, while our coffee beans are carefully selected to provide that perfect morning brew. Step in, relax, and let us serve you a taste of home.
